Summary

I am a theoretical physicist with interests in gravitation and cosmology. I have devoted a substancial part of my work to study the Primordial Universe and Quantum Cosmology. I received my B. S. in Physics (2001) from the Pontifical Catholic University of Rio de Janeiro, PUC-RJ, and my master (2004) and Ph. D. degrees (2008) in Quantum Cosmology from the Brazilian Center for Research in Physics, CBPF. Since 2009, I am a permanent researcher at CBPF as member of the Cosmology, Astrophysics and Fundamental Interactions group. My research spread through bounce and inflationary models, cosmological perturbations, inhomogeneous cosmological models, alternative theories of gravity and non-linear electrodynamics.
